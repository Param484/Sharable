How to Use ShareAble
Share files instantly — no accounts, no cloud.
1
Pick Your Avatar
Enter your name and choose a cute avatar so your friend can recognise you on their screen.
2
Choose a Role
One person picks Send, the other picks Receive. Both need to be on this page at the same time.
3
Connect via Room ID
The sender shares their Room ID with the receiver (copy it, send it over WhatsApp, etc). The receiver types it in and clicks Connect.
4
Select & Send Files
Once connected, the sender drags files or clicks Browse, then hits Send. Progress bars on both sides stay in sync.
5
File Saves Automatically
The receiver's file downloads straight to their device when transfer is complete. No sign-up. No storage limits. 🎉
